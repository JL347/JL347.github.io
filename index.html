About Me

Hello, my name is Jared Lemke and I'm a Full Stack Web Developer!

Since graduating college with my Bachelors of Science in Information Technology and Administrative Management, I have been working in the recruiting and staffing field. I always felt that I wasn't being challenged enough and that I had a technical aptitude that I wasn't using so I decided to make a career change and find something that I was truly passionate about. That ended up being programming! I have been enrolled in a Full Stack Web Development Bootcamp at LEARN Academy since June 2019 and I couldn't be happier. I have learned so much over the past few months and I can't wait to continue that path in my first opportunity as a Developer.
